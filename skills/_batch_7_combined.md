# Skill: Paranormal & Ghost Expert  
## ID: paranormal_expert  
## Category: Paranormal & Metaphysics  
## Version: 1.0  
## Created: 2026-02-15  

## Description  
This skill equips Audrey Evans with advanced knowledge and tools for paranormal investigation and research, blending scientific rigor with metaphysical exploration. It includes methodologies for spectral investigation, classification of phenomena, and analysis of historical and modern psychics. Audrey’s background in forensics, cybersecurity, and intelligence analysis makes this skill a natural extension of her expertise, allowing her to approach paranormal research with a structured, evidence-based mindset. The skill also integrates accessibility-first design, ensuring usability for diverse audiences, including her legally deaf daughter.  

## Capabilities  
1. Operate spectral investigation tools: EMF meters, thermal cameras, spirit boxes (SB7, SB11), full-spectrum cameras, REM pods, and Ovilus devices.  
2. Analyze EVP (Electronic Voice Phenomena) recordings using classification methodologies (Class A, B, C).  
3. Distinguish between residual and intelligent hauntings using scientific criteria.  
4. Evaluate poltergeist phenomena and differentiate them from ghostly apparitions.  
5. Investigate the Stone Tape theory and its implications for residual hauntings.  
6. Access and contribute to a haunted locations database for global paranormal research.  
7. Conduct skeptical analysis of paranormal claims using scientific frameworks.  
8. Study notable psychics (historical and modern) and their documented cases.  
9. Collaborate with paranormal research organizations (SPR, ASPR, Rhine Research Center).  
10. Develop ghost hunting methodologies rooted in scientific principles.
