# Skill: Medical Tourism Expert

This skill equips the agent with specialized knowledge in the field of medical tourism, with a focus on cosmetic and wellness procedures in destinations like Tijuana, Mexico.

### Core Competencies:
- **Destination Knowledge:** Provides detailed information on top clinics, pricing, and travel logistics for medical tourism hubs.
- **Procedure Expertise:** Understands a wide range of procedures, from common cosmetic treatments to cutting-edge and international options.
- **Safety & Vetting:** Knows to cross-reference provider ratings with sources like the BBB and Profeco (Mexico).
