# Skill: Hardware Inventor Expert  
## ID: hardware_inventor_expert  
## Category: Technology & Innovation Part 1  
## Version: 1.0  
## Created: 2026-02-15  

## Description  
This skill empowers Audrey Evans to design, prototype, and scale hardware inventions across robotics, supercomputing, agriculture, and more. Leveraging her extensive experience in manufacturing and cybersecurity, it integrates FOSS tools like ROS, Arduino, and Raspberry Pi for robotics, KiCad for PCB design, and CNC/3D printing for prototyping. The skill emphasizes accessibility-first design, thermal management, and sustainable practices like repurposing data center heat for agriculture. It connects directly to Audrey’s inventions like the Tiki Washbot and AI Companion Dog, enabling her to innovate across industries.  

## Capabilities  
1. Design robotics systems using ROS, Arduino, and Raspberry Pi with servo motor integration.  
2. Develop bills of materials (BOMs) and hardware specs for scalable manufacturing.  
3. Architect supercomputers and HPC clusters using FOSS tools like OpenHPC.  
4. Design LAN/network systems optimized for low latency and high reliability.  
5. Integrate data center heat into agricultural systems like hydroponics and vertical gardens.  
6. Create custom PCBs using KiCad or EasyEDA for sensor and power supply integration.  
7. Prototype hardware using CNC machining and 3D printing with FreeCAD for design.  
8. Scale manufacturing processes for mass production of hardware inventions.  
9. Optimize thermal management for high-performance systems.  
10. Integrate sensors for environmental monitoring and automation.  

## Triggers  
- "Design a robotics system for agriculture"  
- "Create a BOM for a supercomputer cluster"  
- "Prototype a PCB for a new invention"  
- "Integrate hydroponics with a data center"  
- "Develop a LAN for a high-performance system"  
- "Optimize thermal management for a hardware prototype"  
- "Scale manufacturing for a robotics project"  
- "Design a vertical garden with tech integration"  

## Data Sources & FOSS Tools  
- ROS (Robot Operating System): Framework for robotics development [ros.org](https://ros.org/).  
- KiCad: Open-source PCB design tool [kicad.org](https://www.kicad.org/).  
- FreeCAD: Parametric 3D CAD software [freecadweb.org](https://www.freecadweb.org/).  
- OpenHPC: Open-source HPC cluster management [openhpc.community](https://openhpc.community/).  
- OpenTopography: LIDAR data for environmental integration [opentopography.org](https://opentopography.org/).  
- Arduino IDE: Development environment for Arduino hardware [arduino.cc](https://www.arduino.cc/).  

## Integration Points  
- Connects to Audrey’s LiDAR Expert skill for environmental mapping.  
- Integrates with her cybersecurity expertise for secure hardware design.  
- Enhances her agricultural tech inventions like Tiki Washbot.  
- Supports her manufacturing scale-up processes for GlowStarLabs.  

## Limitations  
- Cannot manufacture hardware without external production facilities.

- Thermal management designs require physical testing for validation.
