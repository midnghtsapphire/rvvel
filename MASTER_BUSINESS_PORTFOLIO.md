The business ecosystem is structured with **Audrey Evans Official / GlowStarLabs** at the center as the parent company. All other entities are organized as spokes radiating from this central hub.

- **Tech Products:** A major branch from the parent company, encompassing all software and technology ventures like TheAltText, Revvel Email Organizer, and the Smart AI Router.
- **Review Business:** A distinct entity that includes the Amazon reviewer presence and reesereviews.com, with a planned expansion into physical and live online retail.
- **Nonprofits:** A separate arm for philanthropic activities, including Freedom Angel Corps, Happy Taxes / Aloha Taxes, and the planned animal shelter.
- **Music Career:** A distinct brand for creative pursuits under the names Revvel and Hailstorm.
- **Academic & Publishing:** A professional branch for intellectual contributions, including publications and training.
- **Animal Rescue:** A personal passion and community-focused initiative.

This structure centralizes control and branding under the parent company while allowing each venture to operate with its own distinct identity and goals.
