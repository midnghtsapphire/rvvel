### Career Path in Mechatronics Tooling Engineering and Technicians
Mechatronics tooling engineering combines mechanical engineering, electronics, computer science, and control systems to design, build, and maintain automated tools, machinery, and systems used in manufacturing, robotics, and precision engineering. Tooling engineers focus on creating dies, molds, fixtures, and automated equipment, often integrating sensors and software for smart manufacturing. Technicians (techs) handle installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting of these systems.
