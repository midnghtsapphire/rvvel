Many are designed for ADHD and autism, with some gamification elements. Here's a curated list of top matches based on current options:

- **Tiimo**: A visual planner built for neurodivergent users (ADHD, autism). It tracks routines, tasks, and distractions with low-clutter interfaces, timers, and customizable checklists. - **Lunatask**: An ADHD-friendly task manager that combines to-do lists, habit tracking, mood journaling, and goal setting. It helps spot disruptive patterns and regulate emotions through simple analytics. Light gamification via progress stats. Integrates calendar and notes for life/work balance.
- **Saner.AI**: An AI-powered assistant for neurodivergent adults, focusing on notes, tasks, focus modes, and organization. It can track finances via integrations and flag distractions or loops with AI insights. No Oz theme, but Toto-like AI helper vibe.

- **Inflow**: ADHD-specific app with mood tracking, habit building, and education on emotional regulation. Includes community challenges for bravery/confidence boosts. Gamified with streaks and levels.
